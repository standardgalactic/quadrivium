CREATING CUSTOM
CHATGPT MODELS FOR
CYBERSECURITY TASKS
Custom ChatGPT
models are essential
to address your
organization's unique
vulnerabilities and
challenges effectively.
The true value of custom ChatGPT models is
realized when they are seamlessly integrated
into your cybersecurity workflows.
Begin by defining your cybersecurity objectives
and scope. Collect and annotate relevant data,
and invest in model training to teach it to
recognize and respond to specific threats and
scenarios.
