CONDUCTING COMPLIANCE
AUDITS WITH CHATGPT
Compliance audits serve three fundamental
purposes - identifying gaps in compliance,
verifying 
adherence 
to 
data 
protection
regulations, 
and 
providing 
insights 
for
continuous improvement.
ChatGPT 
can 
play 
a 
valuable 
role 
in
compliance audits.
To effectively prepare for compliance audits,
organizations 
should 
document 
their
policies, 
ensure 
employee 
training 
and
awareness, map their data, and have a
robust incident response plan in place.
