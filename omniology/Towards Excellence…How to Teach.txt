Teachers and Human Values:

Educare:

you are a human being means that human values are latent in you. However, the degree of manifestation

only; but, when this steel undergoes refinement and gets converted into a beautiful wrist watch, it will 
cost a few hundreds of rupees. Likewise, purpose of education is to provide the necessary refinement 
and increase the worth of the individual.

it, you have to reach down to it by digging a well. Education is the process of digging, so that the latent 
‚Äòsweet‚Äô human values are brought to the surface for the benefit of the individual and the society at large.

are under our care. It is a great responsibility to send out these students as highly responsible, upright, 
refined human beings in to the society, who will take our country to great heights of glory.
