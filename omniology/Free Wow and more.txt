Preparing 
In order to get these free games you will need the following: 
1) A valid e-mail address 
2) A battle.net account with country set as Netherlands 
3) 5 minutes of common sense 
4) A proxy. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 

 
Why a proxy? 
Why would you want a proxy? Simple, you don’t want to get IP 
banned by Battle.NET for getting free games. 
 
Getting the free games 
First login on your battle.net account. After that press on Games 
& Codes once the menu pop-ups press on Buy Digital Games. 
 
Now select a game, I will be doing Diablo II. 
 
 
Press on Buy Digital Version and go to the payment page. 
Now follow the image. 

 
Once you are done, continue the order and you will have your 
game!
