Tuple Operations
●
Iteration
>>> days = ('Monday','Tuesday', 'Wednesday', 'Thursday', 'Friday', 'Saturday', 'Sunday')
>>> for day in days:
print(day, end=' ')
Monday Tuesday Wednesday Thursday Friday Saturday Sunday 
●
Concatenation
>>> t1 = (1,2,3)
>>> t2 = (4, 5)
>>> t3 = t1 + t2
>>> t3
(1, 2, 3, 4, 5)
●
Slicing
>>> days[0:3]
('Monday', 'Tuesday', 'Wednesday')
●
Repetition
>>> t2 * 2
(4, 5, 4, 5)
