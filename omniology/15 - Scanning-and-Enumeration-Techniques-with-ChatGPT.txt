SCANNING AND ENUMERATION
TECHNIQUES WITH CHATGPT
Scanning and enumeration in ethical hacking
begin with reconnaissance. It involves gathering
initial information about the target, such as IP
addresses and domains, to create a foundation
for further investigation.
ChatGPT can provide insights into responsible
practices, respecting privacy and consent while
identifying vulnerabilities and strengthening
cybersecurity.
ChatGPT can assist in crafting targeted queries,
selecting appropriate port scanning techniques,
and brainstorming enumeration strategies.
