1. A 10 year old boy is diagnosed with type I diabetes mellitus. Can a drug 
which inhibits the ATP sensitive K channel be used to treat his condition? 
 
A. Yes. Inhibition of the ATP gated K channel increases insulin secretion.
 
B. No. Inhibition of the ATP gated K channel decreases insulin secretion.
 
C. No. The target pancreatic beta cells are missing in this condition
Correct Answer: C. No. The target pancreatic beta cells are missing in this condition
