INTRODUCTION TO NATURAL
LANGUAGE PROCESSING
AND CHATGPT
Natural Language
Processing (NLP) is
the technology that
enables machines
to understand and
generate human
language.
While 
NLP 
and 
ChatGPT 
offer 
significant
advantages in the realm of ethical hacking, their
use comes with ethical and legal responsibilities.
ChatGPT, can automate routine security tasks,
craft convincing social engineering messages,
analyze threat intelligence data, and assist in
anomaly 
detection 
within 
conversations,
enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of
cybersecurity efforts.
