6.2 Hypothalaumus-Posterior Pituitary Axis 
 
1. With profuse sweating (water loss only), the plasma levels of _______ increase. 
A. atrial natriuretic peptide (ANP)  
B. antidiuretic hormone (ADH) 
C. angiotensin II 
D. oxytocin 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Correct Answer 1: B. antidiuretic hormone (ADH)
