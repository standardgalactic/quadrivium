UNDERSTANDING GDPR, HIPAA,
 AND OTHER REGULATIONS
Regulations like GDPR and HIPAA are crucial for
safeguarding individuals' privacy and ensuring
responsible data handling.
While regulations may vary, they share common
themes such as consent, data minimization,
security measures, data subject rights, and the
need for maintaining records of data processing
activities.
Understanding specific regulations such as
GDPR, HIPAA, and CCPA is essential. Each has its
unique focus and penalties for non-compliance.
