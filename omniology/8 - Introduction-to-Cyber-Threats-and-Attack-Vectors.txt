INTRODUCTION TO CYBER THREATS
 AND ATTACK VECTORS
Ethical hackers play
a vital role in
building cyber
resilience by
identifying
vulnerabilities and
anticipating threats.
Attack vectors provide malicious actors with
entry points for unauthorized access. From
social engineering to malware and supply chain
attacks, understanding these vectors is crucial
for ethical hackers.
