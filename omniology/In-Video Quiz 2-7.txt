The Senses – Hearing and the vestibular system
1. The volume of a sound is conveyed to the brain through:
 
A. the portion of the basilar membrane which vibrates
 
B. the amount of ﬂuid that leaves the cochlea
 
C. the frequency of action potential production
2. The response of the otoliths of the utricle during deceleration is similar to 
leaning the head in which direction?
 
A. forward
 
B. backward
 
C. to the right
 
D. to the left
Answer 1: C. the frequency of action potential production
Answer 2: A. forward
