SECURING CONTAINERS AND
SERVERLESS ARCHITECTURES
Containers and serverless architectures offer
scalability and efficiency but come with
unique security challenges due to rapid
adoption, microservices complexity, and the
shared responsibility model.
ChatGPT can provide insights into serverless
function security, secure configuration, and
effective logging and monitoring solutions.
To secure containers effectively, focus on
image security by sourcing images from
trusted registries and regularly updating
them.
