Comparison Operators
Comparison operators compare two operands and yield an boolean value.
There are the following comparison operators:
 == (equal)
 != (not equal)
 < (less)
<= (less or equal)
 > (greater)
>= (greater or equal)

Logical Operators
Logical operators apply to boolean values and yield a result of the same type as the operands 
(bool).
There are 3 logical operators in Go:
 && (conditional and)
 || (conditional or)
 ! (not or logical negation)
