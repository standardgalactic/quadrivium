The Respiratory System – 8, Exercise and hypoxia
1. Putting a tourniquet around someone’s arm will cause which type of hypoxia?
 
A. hypoxic hypoxia
 
B. anemic hypoxia
 
C. ischemic hypoxia
 
D. histotoxic hypoxia
2. During maximal exercise, which of the following is the cause of minute 
ventilation increasing so rapidly?
 
A. low PaO2
 
B. high PaCO2
 
C. increased blood pH
 
D. decreased blood pH
Correct Answer 1: C. ischemic hypoxia
Correct Answer 2: D. decreased blood pH
