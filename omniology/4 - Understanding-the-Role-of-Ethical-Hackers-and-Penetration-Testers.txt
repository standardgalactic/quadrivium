UNDERSTANDING THE ROLE
OF ETHICAL HACKERS AND
PENETRATION TESTERS
Ethical hackers, also
known as "white-hat
hackers," and
penetration testers
are essential
defenders in the
digital realm. 
Ethical hacking has a significant real-world
impact, reducing the cost of cybercrime and
securing critical data. It offers dynamic and
fulfilling career prospects, with opportunities
across various industries.
These professionals possess a unique blend
of skills, including coding, networking, and
using specialized tools.
