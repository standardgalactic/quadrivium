Transporters, Channels, and Pumps
1. Fatty acids enter the cells lining the small intestine by:
 
A. Simple diffusion
 
B. Facilitated diffusion 
 
C. Active transport
2. The PepT is a H+/peptide antiporter located in the small intestine. It has 
which of the following characteristics? 
 
A. requires ATP for activity
 
B. exhibits maximal transport when saturated
 
C. requires H+ and peptides for activity 
 
D. B and C
 
E. A, B, and C
Answer 1: A. Simple diffusion
Answer 2: D. B and C
