A mother and her son are arguing, because he doesn't
want to eat vegetables.
Mother: Eat it all up!
Son: No, I don't like vegetables, I want pizza.
Mother: Well, today there's vegetable soup and you're
not leaving until you eat it. Do you know that pizzas
also have vegetables?
Son: It's a lie!
Mother: Pizzas have tomato, pepper, aubergine,
asparagus…
Son: And what's in this soup?
Mother: It has carrot, pumpkin, courgette and potato.
If you eat it, you'll become big and strong.
Son: Well, today I'll eat it, because I'm hungry.
