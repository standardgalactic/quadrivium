Question: How do ideas spread?

Answer:
 Ideas can spread through both simple and complex contagions. In the case of simple contagion, information is easily spread out in many places, and people only need to hear about something from one friend before they adopt it. On the other hand, complex contagions require multiple doses of influence to spread, often through social networks. Additionally, ideas can spread via social influence, where one person's behavior impacts another's. This can lead to the idea becoming more popular and spreading widely.
