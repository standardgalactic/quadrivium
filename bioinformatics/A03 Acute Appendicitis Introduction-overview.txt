In Lecture 1, the topic of acute appendicitis is introduced as one of the most commonly encountered diseases in medical practice. The lecture will cover several key areas: 

1. **Incidence**: Discussing how widespread the condition is globally.
2. **Clinical Presentation**: Describing how patients typically present with symptoms.
3. **Investigations**: Outlining which diagnostic tests are recommended.
4. **Management**: Explaining the appropriate treatment strategies for acute appendicitis.

The session aims to provide a comprehensive overview of this prevalent medical condition.
