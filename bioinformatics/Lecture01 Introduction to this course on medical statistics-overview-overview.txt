This course on medical statistics is designed for clinicians to learn statistical methods using Microsoft Excel due to its user-friendly nature. The main goals are to help clinicians understand medical literature better by assessing methodologies and p-values in journal articles, and to encourage them to conduct their own research using accessible tools like Excel. By simplifying the application of statistics, the course aims to make research participation easier and more appealing for clinicians, emphasizing that learning statistics is not as difficult as it may seem.
