This lecture series on perioperative management, presented by Jean Klopper from the University of Cape Town, targets both medical students and professionals. It covers three primary areas over three weeks:

1. **Week 1: Pre-operative Evaluation** - Focuses on evaluating patients with cardiovascular conditions, pulmonary issues, diabetes, and those receiving anticoagulation therapy.

2. **Week 2: Direct Perioperative Period** - Discusses fluid management, blood and blood products usage, and prophylactic measures during surgery.

3. **Week 3: Post-operative Complications** - Explores complications such as wound infections, ileus, and hospital-acquired infections like pneumonia and UTIs.

The series aims to provide thorough insights into perioperative care.
