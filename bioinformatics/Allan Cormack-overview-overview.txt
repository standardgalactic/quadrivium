In this episode of "Let's Talk Surgery," Chris Barnard is recognized for performing the first heart transplant at Groote Schuur Hospital in Cape Town, marking a significant milestone in medicine. The discussion then shifts to Alan Cormack, who developed the mathematical foundations for the CT scanner, earning him a Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine in 1979 with Godfrey Hounsfield. Born in Johannesburg and educated at the University of Cape Town, Cormack's contributions have greatly influenced modern medical imaging.
