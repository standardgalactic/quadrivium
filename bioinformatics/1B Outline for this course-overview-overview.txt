The course offers a textbook as an optional resource, but lectures may not adhere strictly to its content. The instructor aims to provide a variety of resources to enrich learning. A humorous example will be used to illustrate the application of limits in life sciences, sparking interest among students. Participants will progressively develop their understanding of using limits with guided instruction. By course end, students are expected to find the subject both intriguing and straightforward. Additionally, the instructor plans to introduce a computer algebra system, teaching how to install and use it for simplifying mathematical calculations through coding.
