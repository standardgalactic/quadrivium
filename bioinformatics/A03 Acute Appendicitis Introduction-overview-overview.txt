In Lecture 1, the topic of acute appendicitis is introduced as a commonly encountered disease in medical practice. The lecture covers several key areas: 

1. **Incidence**: It discusses the global prevalence of the condition.
2. **Clinical Presentation**: It describes typical symptoms patients experience.
3. **Investigations**: It outlines recommended diagnostic tests.
4. **Management**: It explains appropriate treatment strategies for acute appendicitis.

The session aims to provide a comprehensive overview of this prevalent medical condition.
